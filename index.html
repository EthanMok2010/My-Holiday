A Meaningful Holiday

Overseas vacation to Perth,Australia

In September 2017, my parents and I flew to Singapore to spend a night there at my uncle’s house. After that, my family and I went to Perth, Australia and it was towards end of winter season so the weather was very cool. The trip was really fun. We ate lots of nice food and had a great experience there. I saw a big statue that was really tall. It also has big cities and small villages near the sea. The people that stayed in the village near the sea usually go fishing beside their house. I had lots of fun and I hope I can go back to Australia.

Australia, Perth

I like Perth because they have a variety of food and have a lot of nice people. The food they serve is delicious. Their seas are very clean and huge. They are well-known for their “Perth Zoo” and their winery. There are also a lot of special animals like the koala, kangaroo and quokka.
